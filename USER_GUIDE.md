## Getting Started

### What is FeedX HR?
FeedX HR is a platform that helps organizations manage employee performance, set goals, collect feedback, and gain AI-powered insights into workforce productivity.

### Three Types of Users
FeedX HR has three different user roles, each with its own features:

| Role | What You Can Do |
|------|----------------|
| **HR** | Manage employees and managers, view organization-wide analytics, trigger feedback sessions, add new team members |
| **Manager** | Oversee your team, assign goals, request feedback, track team performance |
| **Employee** | View your assigned goals, complete them, respond to feedback requests, see your performance insights |

> **Note:** Your role is assigned when your account is created by your organization's HR team. If you're unsure what role you have, contact your HR administrator.

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Navigate to the Signup Page
1. Open your web browser and go to the FeedX HR website URL
2. Click the **"Sign Up"** or **"Create Account"** button on the homepage

### Step 2: Fill in Your Details
You'll see a form with the following fields:

1. **Full Name** — Enter your complete name (e.g., "John Doe")
2. **Email** — Enter your work email address (this will be used for login and verification)
3. **Password** — Create a strong password (use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols)
4. **Organization Type**:
   - **Existing Organization**: Select this if your company already uses FeedX HR. You'll need to enter your **Organization ID** (ask your HR team for this)
   - **New Organization**: Select this if you're setting up FeedX HR for your company for the first time. Enter your **Organization Name**

### Step 3: Submit the Form
- Click **"Create Account"**
- You'll be redirected to the **Email Verification** page

### Step 4: Verify Your Email
1. Check your email inbox for a verification code (OTP - One-Time Password)
2. Enter the 6-digit code on the verification page
3. Click **"Verify"**
4. Once verified, you'll be logged in automatically

> **Tip:** If you don't see the email, check your spam or junk folder. The code may take up to 2 minutes to arrive.

## Logging In

### How to Log In
1. Go to the FeedX HR website
2. Click **"Login"**
3. Enter your **email address** and **password**
4. Click **"Login"**
5. You'll be redirected to your role-specific dashboard

### Forgot Password?
If you can't remember your password, contact your HR administrator. Currently, password resets must be handled manually by your organization's HR team.

## Understanding Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll land on your personalized dashboard. This is your central hub for all activities.

### Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard displays:

- **Welcome Message** — Greets you by name
- **Key Metrics** — Quick stats relevant to your role (e.g., goals assigned, completion rates, feedback scores)
- **Charts & Graphs** — Visual representations of performance data
- **AI Insights** — Smart recommendations powered by artificial intelligence
- **Recent Activity** — Latest goals, feedback, and notifications
- **Notification Bell** (🔔) — Shows unread notifications

### Navigation Menu
On the left side of the screen, you'll find a sidebar menu with links to different sections:

| Menu Item | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your main overview page |
| **Employees / Managers** | View and manage team members (HR & Manager roles) |
| **Goals** | View, create, and manage goals |
| **Feedbacks** | Manage feedback requests and responses |
| **Profile** | Update your personal information |

## Working with Goals

### What Are Goals?
Goals are specific objectives assigned to employees. They help track progress and measure performance.

### Viewing Your Goals
1. Click **"Goals"** in the sidebar menu
2. You'll see a list of all your goals with their status:
   - **Pending** — Not yet completed
   - **In Progress** — Currently being worked on
   - **Completed** — Successfully finished

### For Managers: Assigning a Goal
1. Navigate to **Goals**
2. Click **"Add Goal"** or **"Create Goal"**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title** — A short name for the goal
   - **Description** — Detailed explanation of what needs to be achieved
   - **Assignee** — Select the employee(s) this goal is for
   - **Deadline** — Set a due date
   - **Priority** — Mark as Low, Medium, or High
4. Click **"Create"** to assign the goal

### For Employees: Updating Goal Status
1. Go to **Goals**
2. Find the goal you want to update
3. Change the status from **Pending** to **Completed** once you've finished
4. Add any notes or comments if needed

## Managing Feedback & Feedback Requests

### What is 360° Feedback?
360° feedback allows employees to receive input from multiple sources—peers, managers, and self-assessments—providing a complete picture of performance.

### Viewing Feedback Requests
1. Click **"Feedbacks"** in the sidebar
2. You'll see:
   - **Pending Requests** — Feedback you need to give or respond to
   - **Completed Feedback** — Past feedback you've provided or received

### Responding to a Feedback Request
1. Click on the feedback request
2. Read the questions or criteria provided
3. Fill in your responses honestly and thoughtfully
4. Choose a rating if applicable (e.g., 1-5 stars)
5. Click **"Submit"**

> **Note:** Some feedback may be anonymous, depending on your organization's settings.

### For HR/Managers: Triggering a Feedback Session
1. Navigate to **Feedbacks** or **Trigger Feedback**
2. Select the employee(s) involved
3. Choose the feedback type (e.g., peer review, self-assessment)
4. Set a deadline for responses
5. Click **"Create"** to send out the requests

## Notifications

### What Are Notifications?
Notifications alert you to important events, such as:
- New goals assigned to you
- Feedback requests requiring your response
- Goal deadline reminders
- Feedback you've received

### Checking Notifications
1. Click the **Notification Bell** (🔔) in the top right corner
2. A dropdown will show your recent notifications
3. Click any notification to view details

### Notification Badges
- A **red dot** or **number** on the bell means you have unread notifications
- Once you view them, the badge will disappear

## Troubleshooting

### I Can't Log In
- **Double-check your email and password** — Ensure there are no typos
- **Caps Lock is off** — Passwords are case-sensitive
- **Contact HR** — If you still can't log in, your HR administrator may need to reset your account

### I Didn't Receive the OTP Email
- **Wait 2 minutes** — Sometimes emails are delayed
- **Check spam/junk folder** — The email may have been filtered
- **Verify your email address** — Ensure you entered the correct email during signup
- **Contact HR** — They can resend the verification email

### I Can't See Certain Menu Items
Your menu options depend on your **role**. If you believe you're missing access to a feature you should have, contact your HR administrator to verify your role permissions.

### Charts Aren't Loading
- **Refresh the page** — Sometimes a simple reload fixesifies the issue
- **Check your internet connection** — Dashboards require an active connection to fetch data
- **Clear your browser cache** — In your browser settings, clear cached data and reload

### I Get an Error Message
- **Read the error carefully** — It often tells you what went wrong (e.g., "Email already exists")
- **Try again** — Some errors are temporary
- **Note the error text** — If the problem persists, share the exact message with your HR or IT support
